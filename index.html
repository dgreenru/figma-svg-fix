Figma online SVG fixer

If you are creating SVG graphics in tools e.g. Affinity Designer, then you might encounter incorrect masks when importing graphics into Figma. You also may have issues with 1x1 pixel container. The OK.RU team has prepared a script for you that will help solve this problem.
